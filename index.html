About the Game

ULTRAKILL is a fast-paced ultraviolent retro first-person shooter. Combine powerful weaponry with fast movement to tear through hordes of the damned in a descent through the layers of Hell.

Blood is fuel — heal yourself by bathing in the blood of your enemies. The faster you move, the harder you hit, the more you heal. Keep the momentum or die.

Blood-Fueled

Damage enemies up close to heal. Aggression is rewarded — standing still is death.

Arsenal

Revolvers, shotguns, nailguns, and railcannons — each with alternate fire modes and techniques.

Movement

Dash, slide, slam, and walljump. Chain movement abilities for maximum speed and style.

Style Scoring

Earn style ranks from D to ULTRAKILL. Vary your attacks and keep the combo going.
